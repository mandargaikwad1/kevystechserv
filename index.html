Document Scanning Journey

  • Step 1:Collection

    Internally managed fleet of tracked vehicles. Flatpacks & packing services provided.Unbranded vehicles for added security.

  • Step 2:Document Perparation

    Boxes booked onto our internal tracking system. Removal of paperclips, staples, and bindings.Pre-scan checks performed.

  • Step 3:Document Scanning

    Documents Are scanned in B&W or Color.Dpi adjusted to maintain quality. Scanned in accordance with British Standard BIP0008.

  • Step 4:Image Preparation

    Post scan quality checks. Digital images exported to any format (PDF, TIFF, JPEG).Tailored indexing and OCR application.

  • Step 5:Access

    KEVYSDOCS document management.2019 Document Management Product of the Year. Quick transfer via SFTP / encrypted hard-drive.

  • Step 6:Document Shredding

    100% of paper waste recycled.Shredding certificate provided.

  • Step 7:Document Storage

    Document Storage for years and decades.High-security archive storage facility. Access to paper originals indefinitely.
